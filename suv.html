SUV

SUV stands for Sports Utility Vehicle, a popular category of automobiles known for their combination of power, comfort, and versatility. Unlike regular cars, SUVs are built with a higher ground clearance, robust suspension systems, and spacious interiors, making them suitable for both city driving and off-road adventures. Modern SUVs often come with advanced safety features, luxury interiors, and hybrid or electric powertrains, attracting families as well as adventure enthusiasts. Globally, SUVs dominate the automotive market because they balance practicality, style, and performance.

MERCEDES

The Mercedes-Benz G-Wagon (short for Gelandewagen, meaning “cross-country vehicle” in German) is a luxury SUV that combines rugged off-road capability with high-end style. First launched in 1979, the G-Wagon was originally designed as a military and utility vehicle, but over the years it evolved into a global status symbol.

LAND ROVER

The Land Rover Defender is a legendary British SUV, famous for its rugged design, durability, and outstanding off-road capability. First introduced in 1948, it was originally designed as a practical utility vehicle, but over time it became an icon of adventure and exploration.

RANGE ROVER

The Range Rover is a luxury SUV made by Land Rover, well known for blending off-road capability with premium comfort and style. First launched in 1970, the Range Rover has become a symbol of prestige and performance worldwide.
